HOW IT WORKS

Our website takes in data of all U of M players looking for a game. Based on the partner preferences and time of availability, we match people and share their contact info with each other.

They can message each other directly, to fix the exact time and place to play.

Members are matched based on age, gender, and skill level and time of availability.
